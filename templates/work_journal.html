  1. Software Side
    1. Lojban-Translator
      • It feels like a school project. Tried their examples and one throws an exception.

      • The flags you can set are not that impressive. –tree simply prints the tree generated by the camxes package. -c prints the entire hard-coded dictionary, and -g prints the gismu dictionary.
